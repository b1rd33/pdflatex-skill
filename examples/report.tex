\section{Executive Summary}
\label{sec:summary}

Q1 2026 delivered strong results across all business units. Revenue grew 18\% year-over-year to \$47.3M, exceeding our target of \$44M. Key highlights:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Revenue}: \$47.3M (+18\% YoY), driven by Enterprise segment growth
    \item \textbf{Gross Margin}: 72.4\%, up from 68.1\% in Q1 2025
    \item \textbf{Customer Acquisition}: 342 new accounts, including 28 enterprise deals
    \item \textbf{Net Revenue Retention}: 124\%, indicating strong expansion within existing accounts
    \item \textbf{Headcount}: 847 employees (+62 net new hires)
\end{itemize}

Challenges remain in the SMB segment where churn increased to 4.2\% monthly, and in APAC where macro headwinds slowed pipeline velocity by 15\%. Mitigation strategies are outlined in \cref{sec:outlook}.

\subsection{Margin Analysis}

Gross margin improved to 72.4\% from 68.1\%, primarily driven by:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Infrastructure cost optimization (migrated 40\% of workloads to spot instances)
    \item Improved pricing on new Enterprise contracts (+8\% average deal size)
    \item Reduced support cost per account through self-service tooling
\end{enumerate}

Operating expenses grew 12\% YoY, below revenue growth of 18\%, demonstrating improving operating leverage. R\&D spend as a percentage of revenue declined from 32\% to 29\%.
